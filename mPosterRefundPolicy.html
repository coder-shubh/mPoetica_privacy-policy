Refund Policy for Plan Subscription

Effective Date: 16 July 2025

1. General Policy

All subscription plans are billed in advance on a recurring basis. By subscribing, you agree to our billing cycle and refund terms.

2. Refund Eligibility

Monthly Subscriptions

  • Refunds are not provided for partial months or unused time.
  • You may cancel anytime, and the service remains active until the end of the billing period.

Annual or Long-Term Subscriptions

  • Refunds are not provided for partial months or unused time.
  • You may cancel anytime, and the service remains active until the end of the billing period.

3. Free Trials

  • No charges during the trial period.
  • Cancel before the trial ends to avoid charges.
  • No refunds after the trial converts to a paid plan.

4. Special Circumstances

We may consider refund requests beyond the standard policy for cases like accidental duplicate purchases or technical issues. These must be submitted within 30 days of the charge.

5. How to Request a Refund

Email us at shiv@mavesys.com with:

  • Your account details
  • Reason for the request
  • Date of the charge

6. Chargebacks

Please contact us first with any billing issues. Chargebacks may result in account suspension.

Note: This policy is subject to change. Continued use of our services after updates indicates acceptance.
